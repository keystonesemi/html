KSW8290 Overview
KSW8290 IC is the first DAB/DAB+/FM Module in a chip. KSW8290 integrates a tri-band RF tuner, DAB/DAB+/FM baseband processor, MP2/AAC+ audio decoder, FLASH memory, Audio DAC, and power management functions into one single BGA chip. The size of the BGA chip is 8mm*11mm. Only few passive components are needed to make a complete low-power DAB receiver. The high integration, minimum components, and low power had made KSW8290 an ideal add-on feature for portable MP3/Cellphone product, in addition to the traditional kitchen radio and car radio product.

Features
 ˙ Complete DAB/DAB+/FM radio receiver in a chip
 ˙ Integrate low power tri-band RF tuner, DAB/DAB+/FM baseband processor, Audio              decoder, and power management on chip
 ˙ Integrate 1MB FLASH on chip
 ˙ Comply to ETSI specification 300 401, and ETSI TS 102 563
 ˙ Support all DAB modes (I, II, III, and IV)
 ˙ Reception of Band-3 and L-Band, and FM Band
 ˙ DAB sensitivity to -100dBm (BAND-3), FM to 2uV (typical)
 ˙ Support RDS: Time and Date, Radio Text, and program type
 ˙ On-chip DAB+ audio decoder, with certified audio quality and error concealment
 ˙ On-chip Audio DAC with headphone amplifier
 ˙ I2C and UART port to control/receive external devices
 ˙ Support master mode (standalone system)
 ˙ LCD display interface (16×2 characters LCM)
 ˙ Keyboard controller for 4x4 key matrix
 ˙ Infrared and thumb-wheel input pre-processing
 ˙ Low power On-chip 32KHz RTC with power up/down control. Support CPU-initiated ˙ power down and RTC-initiated power up to minimize system idle power

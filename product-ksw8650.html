KSW8650 Overview
KSW8650 is an all new DAB/DAB+/DMB/FM Module on a chip, with support for DMB-R, DMB-Video, Color Slide Show, TPEG/TMC, and DLS/DLS+. KSW8650 integrates a tri-band RF tuner and DAB/DAB+/DMB/FM baseband processor, and bit stream pre-processor for DMB-R, DMB-Video, Color Slide Show, TPEG/TMC, DLS/DLS+, and MP2/AAC+ audio decoder, FLASH memory, Audio DAC, and power management functions into one single BGA chip. The size of the BGA chip is 8mm*11mm. Only few passive components are needed to make a complete low-power DAB receiver. The high integration, minimum components, and low power had made KSW8650 an ideal add-on feature for iPhone/iPod dongle or accessory, and GPS or DPF, in addition to the traditional kitchen radio and car radio product. 

Features
 ˙ Complete DAB/DAB+/DMB/FM radio receiver on a chip
 ˙ Integrate low power tri-band RF tuner, DAB/DAB+/DMB/FM baseband processor, and      power management on chip
 ˙ Output DMB-R, DMB-Video, color slide show, DLS/DLS+ and TPEG or TMC bit           stream to external host through SPI or UART
 ˙ Integrate 1MB FLASH on chip
 ˙ Comply to ETSI specification 300 401, and ETSI TS 102 563
 ˙ Support all DAB modes (I, II, III, and IV)
 ˙ Reception of Band-3 and L-Band, and FM Band
 ˙ DAB sensitivity to -100dBm (BAND-3), FM to 2uV (typical)
 ˙ Support RDS: Time and Date, Radio Text, and program type
 ˙ On-chip DAB+ audio decoder, with certified audio quality and error concealment
 ˙ On-chip Audio DAC with headphone amplifier
 ˙ I2C and UART and SPI to control/receive external devices
 ˙ Input power: 3.3V for I/O, 1.8V for RF IC, and 1.2V for baseband core
 ˙ Low power consumption
